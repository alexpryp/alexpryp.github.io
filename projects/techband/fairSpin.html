About company

Fairspin is a cryptocurrency-based online casino that allows customers to fund their accounts with different digital coins and more common payment options. These payment types for depositing funds include Bitcoin, Bitcoin Cash, Ethereum, Dogecoin, Ripple, and Neteller. The withdrawal methods are like the deposits that include various cryptocurrencies, e-wallet options, and bank transfers. Users play anonymously, can check the RTP and monitor the fairness of the game. In October 2019, the creators of the Fair Spin blockchain casino presented the project at the EGR Operator Awards in London. The platform was nominated in the Rising Star category.
