About company

Assembly is a platform designed to empower photographers and the organisations that support them, to inspire collectors with the most important and culturally valuable works of the world's leading fine art photographers, as well as innovative new voices, to create value through access, context, and carefully selected collections. After having been steward fine art photographers in the space since 2021, the team launched the Assembly Curated Platform in 2022 to be a lighthouse in the NFT space, guiding artists, the organisations that support them, and collectors with the most culturally valuable works on the market.

Full smart contract / backend / frontend development

For this project, our team provided a package of services that includes the development of smart contracts (Ethereum Network), backend, frontend, and full coverage of the product with tests. We have developed and released the following products for the project: the creation of a unique EIP721 project token with the supported build, royalty transfer, lazy minting, enhanced multi-author support for different artists and collections, support of both digital and real-world art objects, auction bidding platform, and supporting custodial version of the auctions.
