Liquid Access

About company

Liquid Access is a platform that allows you to operate your subscriptions using blockchain solutions. If you have one of the subscriptions to the services of one of our partners, then you have the opportunity to generate an NFT token that you can transfer or sell after a time when this subscription becomes irrelevant to you. The project also includes a marketplace where you can auction your NFT subscriptions. The project uses the Polygon Network, which allows the project to provide a low gas price for transactions to its users.
