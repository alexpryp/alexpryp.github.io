Bubblegum

About company

Bubblegum is an NFT marketplace that includes a unique NFT collection created that is dedicated to various artists. In this marketplace, there is an auction with the ability to put up for sale both digital art and classic physical paintings. The focus is primarily on high quality physical and digital art/collectibles limited collections that will be vetted by Bubblegum initially and progressing to a community decision.
